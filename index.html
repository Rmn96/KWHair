Meet Katrin Winnet

With over 30 years of experience in all aspects of hairdressing, I offer professional, personalised services from a quiet and private home based salon. My focus is on providing a calm, welcoming environment where clients can relax and enjoy dedicated, one-on-one attention.

Every appointment begins with a thorough consultation. I take the time to listen carefully to what you want, offer honest professional advice, and tailor each haircut or style to suit your hair type, lifestyle, and preferences. My goal is always to deliver the best possible outcome, ensuring you leave feeling confident, comfortable, and happy with your hair.

If you are seeking an experienced hairdresser who values quality, care, and personal service in a peaceful home salon setting, you’re in the right place.

What I Can Do

From classic, timeless cuts to modern, easy-to-wear styles, each look reflects a tailored approach that considers hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. With decades of hands-on experience and a commitment to one-on-one service, KWHair offers clients the confidence of knowing their hair is in skilled, professional hands.
